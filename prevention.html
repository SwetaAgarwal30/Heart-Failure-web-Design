Introduction to Heart Disease Prevention

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death globally. However, many cases of heart disease can be prevented through lifestyle changes and proper management of risk factors. In this article, we'll explore some key strategies for preventing heart disease.

Healthy Diet

A healthy diet plays a crucial role in preventing heart disease. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your diet. Limit saturated fats, trans fats, cholesterol, sodium, and added sugars. Know More

Regular Exercise

Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining heart health. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week. Additionally, incorporate strength training exercises at least two days per week. Know More

Manage Stress

Chronic stress can contribute to heart disease. Practice stress-reduction techniques such as meditation, deep breathing exercises, yoga, or spending time in nature to help manage stress levels. Having a daily schedule can help us use our time efficiently and feel more in control. Set time for regular meals, time with family members, exercise, daily chores and other recreational activities.
